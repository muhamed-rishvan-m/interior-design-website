About company

some history

- 1920

Founded in 1920, emerged with a passion for craftsmanship and attention to detail, bringing beauty into homes through custom furniture and intricate finishes. Known for its commitment to timeless quality, the company quickly gained a reputation as a leader in classic interior design, setting a strong foundation that would carry forward for generations.

- 1986

By 1986, embraced a fresh era of design, blending contemporary styles with its longstanding tradition of excellence. Adapting to the demand for modern, open spaces and sleek aesthetics, the company continued to create inspiring interiors that balanced innovation with a respect for its legacy.
